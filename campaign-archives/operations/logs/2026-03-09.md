## Lessons
- `vm_stat` free pages can dip very low (observed ~56.7MB) even with normal load; alerting should key off **free mem**, not load.
- `scripts/archive-results.sh` can report "Archive Complete" even when the working tree has many modified/deleted/untracked files and nothing is committed—should verify what actually got archived/committed.
